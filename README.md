# 🥗 DormMate AI

> **Cook Smarter. Eat Better. Waste Less.**

DormMate AI is an AI-powered kitchen assistant designed for students living in dormitories or away from home. It helps users prepare affordable, nutritious, and sustainable meals by understanding their dietary needs, budget, cooking preferences, and available ingredients.

Built for the **AMD x LabLab AI Hackathon**, DormMate AI leverages **Gemma** to provide personalized recipe recommendations, intelligent ingredient substitutions, and AI-powered nutrition insights while promoting healthier eating habits and reducing food waste.

---

## The Problem

Students often struggle with:

- Limited food budgets
- Lack of cooking experience
- Food waste from unused ingredients
- Finding recipes that fit dietary restrictions
- Maintaining a healthy diet while living independently

Most recipe applications rely on simple keyword searches, requiring users to already know what they want to cook.

---

## Our Solution

DormMate AI transforms cooking into an intelligent, personalized experience by combining AI reasoning with practical meal planning.

Instead of simply matching recipe names, DormMate AI considers:

- Personal dietary preferences
- Religious requirements
- Allergies and medical conditions
- Nutrition goals
- Budget constraints
- Available pantry ingredients

to recommend meals that are affordable, healthy, and realistic to prepare.

---

## Key Features

- **AI-Personalized Homepage** with dynamic recipe recommendations
- **Natural Language Recipe Search** powered by Gemma
- **Pantry Scanner** that detects available ingredients from an image
- **Smart Ingredient Substitutions** to reduce unnecessary shopping
- **Budget-Aware Meal Recommendations** with estimated recipe costs
- **AI Nutrition Insights** including calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fats
- **Personalized User Profiles** supporting dietary restrictions, allergies, and health goals
- **Saved Recipes** for quick access to favorite meals

## Sustainable Development Goals

DormMate AI contributes to three United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by making healthy cooking more accessible and sustainable.

### SDG 2 — Zero Hunger
- Encourages affordable and nutritious meal planning
- Makes healthy recipes accessible within a student's budget
- Reduces financial barriers to healthy eating

### SDG 3 — Good Health and Well-being
- Delivers personalized meal recommendations based on nutrition goals, allergies, and medical conditions
- Provides AI-generated nutrition insights to encourage healthier food choices

### SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption and Production
- Reduces food waste through pantry scanning
- Suggests ingredient substitutions using available resources
- Encourages smarter grocery purchasing and sustainable cooking habits
